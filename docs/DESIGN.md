# 🧭 Design Document

## Core Idea

You are surviving month-to-month in a broken world.

Not thriving.
Not winning.

Surviving.

---

## 1. Survival Pressure

The player is to remain under pressure:

- hunger increases
- resources run out
- time moves forward

---

## 2. Meaningful Choices

Every action:

- a cost
- a risk
- a consequence

---

## 3. Emergent Storytelling

Stories are not scripted.They emerge from:

- events
- decisions
- consequences

---

## 4. Uncertainty

The player should never feel fully safe.

---

## 5. World

The player should create an imaginative world in their mind while playing, based on the choices they make.
